# Investment Practice

## Current Allocation Strategy
- **Core Holdings (Beta):** Majority allocated to NASDAQ index funds for long-term market growth.
- **Active Management (Alpha):** Selective stock picking only when I possess a distinct informational advantage or deep industry insight.
- **Cash Equivalents (Safe Assets):** Short-term US Treasury bill ETFs (e.g., SGOV) for liquidity and risk-free yield.

## Reference Allocation Models
- **Buffett's Rule:** 90% S&P 500, 10% short-term Treasuries (T-Bills).
- **Modern Barbell:** Stocks + T-Bills (captures equity upside while avoiding long-bond interest rate risk).
- **Risk Parity (Dalio):** Uses long bonds to hedge against stock market crashes.
