# US Accounts Setup Timeline

*Related: [account_allocation](account_allocation.md) - overall strategy for savings, FX, and investing.*

Week 1-2 (after arrival)
  → Apply for SSN at SSA office (after 10+ business days in US)

Week 3-4 (when SSN arrives) — target ~June 4-8
  → Open Chase Total Checking (in branch) — do this in person, online apps often denied without US credit history
  → Apply for Chase Freedom Rise same visit — maximizes credit history length before departure
  → Open Schwab Bank Investor Checking (online)
  → Fund Schwab with ~$100 ACH transfer from Chase
  → Open IBKR US Pro (online, needs SSN + US address) — ~3.14% on USD cash, best FX rates, sets up Norbert's Gambit for return to Canada
  → Fill out W-8BEN when prompted (J-1 = non-resident alien for tax purposes, this is normal)
  → Keep IBKR Canada dormant while in US (jurisdictional grey area); reactivate on return

Week 5-7
  → Apply for Discover It (online)

Month 2-5 (during internship)
  → Use Chase as primary (paychecks, rent, daily spend)
  → Use credit cards for daily spend (pay statement balance in full)
  → Use Schwab debit for any travel/international ATM use
  → Build credit history

Month 4 (pre-departure cleanup)
  → Update Chase + Schwab + Discover addresses to Canadian
  → Set up autopay (statement balance) on both credit cards from Chase
  → Add small recurring charge to each credit card (Netflix, $1/mo cloud, etc.)
  → Set up Google Voice US number (free, forever) for SMS verification
  → Save all account info in password manager

Month 5
  → Leave US

Following spring (from Canada)
  → File 1040-NR + Form 8843 via Sprintax (~$50)
  → Refund deposited to still-active Chase account
