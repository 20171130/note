# Cleaning
- floss, nail clippers, ear pick, cotton swabs, mouthwash
- laundry and dishwasher detergent
- trash bags, toilet tissue
> I tried camera ear picks — not very useful. Ear wax (especially dry wax, typical for Asians like me) is mostly dead skin flakes rather than secretions, distributed along the ear canal instead of packed in one spot. Seeing it does not help much with removal; cotton swabs work better than ear picks.

# Kitchen
- knife, pan, pot
- salt, pepper, soy sauce, ketchup
- slider bags (gallon, quart), chopsticks, plastic wrap, foil, parchment paper
> No cooking oil — boil in water, or use foil for even heat and parchment paper to prevent sticking to the pan. For steak (Maillard): dry the surface, high heat; bake both sides uncovered (not in foil), then reverse-sear in a pan with avocado oil (high smoke point). Maillard browning is essential for flavor but bad for health.
