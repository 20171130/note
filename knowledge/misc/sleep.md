Wearing a sleep mask blocks early sunlight but can cause oversleeping: when the alarm rings during deep sleep, I feel groggy and go back to sleep. Without the mask, sunlight wakes me too early with the same result.

A dawn-simulating mask could help, but [current options are not great](https://www.reddit.com/r/sleep/comments/1mhl9gm/experience_with_sleeping_masks_mimicking_sunrise/).

Current best strategy: use the sleepcycle app so the alarm rings during light sleep, then remove the mask.
